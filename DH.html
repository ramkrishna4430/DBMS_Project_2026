Quick Guide

Use Manage Inventory to add, edit, or delete your products. The TRIGGER: check_min_stock will prevent stock from dropping below minimum levels.
Use Manage Orders to update order statuses and process payments. The TRIGGER: trigger_log_sales automatically logs to sales_history when payment is completed.
Use Sales Report to generate date-range reports via the PROC: generate_sales_report() procedure.

Manage Inventory

Add, edit, or remove products from your catalog. 🔒 TRIGGER: check_min_stock

⚠️
SQL Trigger: check_min_stock
Fires BEFORE UPDATE on the inventory table. If the new stock_quantity would fall below min_stock, the transaction is blocked with an exception. This protects against dangerously low inventory levels.

SQL Trigger: trigger_log_sales
Fires AFTER UPDATE on the payment table. When payment_status changes to 'completed', it automatically loops through all order_item rows and inserts records into the sales_history table, recording each sale.

Sales Report

Generate revenue reports using the database procedure. ⚙️ PROC: generate_sales_report()

📗
SQL Procedure: generate_sales_report(start_date, end_date)
This stored procedure calculates: (1) total revenue from sales_history in the given date range, (2) identifies the top-selling product by quantity. It returns p_total_revenue, p_top_product_name, and p_top_qty_sold.
